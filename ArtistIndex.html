NAME: Fernando Poe Jr


On this day in 1939, Filipino
actor and director Fernando Poe Jr. was born in Manila. He is known as the "King of Philippine Movies"
for starring in film classics like Ang Panday, Asedillo, and Dito sa Pitong Gatang. He was named National Artist for Film in 2006.
